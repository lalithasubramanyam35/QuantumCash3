# Quantum Cash 💸

> "Imagine a weather forecast app, but for a business's bank account. Instead of telling you it's going to rain, our system predicts exactly when your business will run out of money—and then it automatically writes a letter to the bank to get you a raincoat!" 🌧️💼

---

## 📋 The 3-Step Explanation

### 1. The Problem 📉
* Most small businesses do not fail because they are bad.
* **The truth is**, they fail because of bad timing.
* For example, you have to pay suppliers and payroll on Monday, but customers don't pay you until Friday.
* That tiny gap can shut a business down.

### 2. The Prediction 🔮
* Our Python engine acts as a **Virtual Treasurer**.
* It reads daily transactions and forecasts cash flow **7 days into the future**.
* It flags the exact date the balance will dip below zero.

### 3. The AI Rescue ⚡
* Instead of just panicking, the engine automatically triggers **Gemini 3.5 Flash**.
* **The best part?** The AI instantly drafts a highly professional, math-backed micro-loan request tailored for a bank, asking for the exact buffer needed to survive the week.
