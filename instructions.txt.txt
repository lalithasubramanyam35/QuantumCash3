How the Engine Works
generator.py
: Simulates a small business transaction ledger. It models daily sales, daily overheads, weekly supplier payments (Mondays), software subscriptions (Wednesdays), bi-weekly payrolls (every second Friday), and monthly rents (1st of the month). It accepts a --scenario parameter (stable or crunch) to test different cash flows.
predictor.py
: Reads transaction logs from a file or stdin. It computes the running balance, extracts frequency profiles (daily vs. periodic recurring items), and runs a day-by-day cash flow projection over the next 7 days, flagging any cash crunches (balance dipping below 0) with warnings and non-zero exit codes.
