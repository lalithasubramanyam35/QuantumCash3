1. The Problem: The Cash Flow Visibility Gap

Small business failure is rarely caused by a fundamental lack of profitability. Rather, it is driven by timing mismatches. Over 82% of commercial bankruptcies are due to poor cash flow management—often where a business has high-value receivable contracts but experiences a sudden critical liquidity shortfall because supplier expenses, payroll commitments, and utility payments coincide before receipts are cleared. Current legacy commercial banking portals operate in a strictly rearview mode, informing treasurers of what they spent yesterday rather than warning them of shortfalls next week.

2. The Technology: AI-Powered Core Predictive Engine

QuantumCash bridges the visibility gap through a proprietary, automated forecasting engine. The technology operates on three distinct layers:

  • Frequency Extraction Protocol: Processes standard ledger CSV entries to map recurring daily receipts, periodic supplier draws (weekly), SaaS utilities (monthly), and staffing costs (bi-weekly).
  • Automated Forward Forecasting: Computes running balances dynamically over a rolling 7-day window. It detects negative balance intersections (crunches) before they occur.
  • Generative LLM Underwriting: If a cash crunch is imminent, the engine communicates with Google Gemini to automatically draft a professional, mathematically justified loan application letter with precise repayment terms tailored to a banking partner.

3. Commercial Roadmap: 2026 – 2028

We are positioning QuantumCash to become the definitive forecasting and credit bridge middleware for digital banks:

H2 2026

Plaid & Yodlee Integration: Move from file-based synthetic uploads to live read-only ledger polling across multi-account commercial profiles.

H1 2027

Automated Micro-Lending Portal: Establish partner credit routes letting commercial lenders review automated micro-letters with real-time risk scores, cutting loan approval cycles from weeks to seconds.

FY 2028

Enterprise API SDK: Release a highly scalable predictive SaaS endpoint for integration into external small business ERP systems, billing software, and accounting apps.
